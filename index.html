How it works

Three taps. Your record is saved.

No typing. No menus. No paperwork. Just speak what you did, confirm, and it's logged.

  1. 1

    Tap the microphone

    One big button, always on screen. Works one-handed, even mid-inspection with gloves on.

  2. 2

    Speak what you did

    Say what you did — 'Hive 3, oxalic acid, 2ml' — and TerraScribe fills in the details automatically.

  3. 3

    Confirm and save

    Review, correct anything if needed, and tap Save. Your record is permanently logged.
