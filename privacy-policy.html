Privacy Policy — TerraScribe

Last updated: March 4, 2025

1. Who we are

TerraScribe is a mobile application for farmers and beekeepers. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at contact@terrascribe.app.

2. The short version

TerraScribe is private by default. Your farm data never leaves your device unless you deliberately export it. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal or farm data to any server.

3. Data stored on your device

All records you create in TerraScribe — hive inspections, field logs, treatments, harvests, tasks — are stored exclusively on your device using local storage. We have no access to this data. Uninstalling the app deletes it.

4. Data you choose to export

When you use the CSV or PDF export feature, the file is generated on your device. You decide what to do with it. TerraScribe does not send exported files to any server.

5. Contact form data

If you submit the contact form on terrascribe.app, your name, email, and message are sent to our form processing service (Formspree) and forwarded to us by email. We use this only to respond to your enquiry. We do not add you to mailing lists without your consent. We do not share your contact data with third parties.

6. Analytics and tracking

TerraScribe does not currently use any analytics or tracking tools inside the app. Our website may use basic analytics to understand visitor numbers. If we add or change analytics tools, we will update this policy.

7. Push notifications

TerraScribe may send local push notifications for tasks and reminders you create. These are generated entirely on your device and are not processed by any external server.

8. No account required

TerraScribe requires no account or personal information to use. Download and start logging immediately.

9. Children's privacy

TerraScribe is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10. Changes to this policy

We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page if we make significant changes.
